Features

Everything you need

A full-featured ZoneMinder client, built from the ground up.

Live Monitoring

Watch all your cameras in real-time with adaptive streaming. See live feeds, FPS, resolution, and PTZ controls at a glance.

Event Playback

Browse, search, and filter recorded events. View video playback with frame-level detail, timing info, and object detection scores.

Montage View

Multi-camera grid with customizable column layouts. Choose from 1 to 5 columns or set your own custom arrangement.

Push Notifications

Get instant alerts on motion detection events. Filter by monitor, configure notification preferences, and view full notification history.

Multi-Server Profiles

Manage multiple ZoneMinder servers from a single app. Switch between home, office, or client installations with one tap.

Themes & Languages

Multiple built-in themes including light, dark, slate, and amber. Available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Chinese.
